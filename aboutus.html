About Us


As students specializing in IT technology at Olympic College, we have embarked on a project to enhance support for our fellow military-connected students and their families. Our objective is to develop a simple platform that effectively broadcasts essential services while also optimizing inventory management for MVP/SVA (Military and Veterans Program/Students of America) employees.


This initiative serves a dual purpose: first, it provides a centralized hub where military-connected students and their families can easily access vital resources and assistance tailored to their unique needs, thereby enhancing their convenience. Second, it aims to streamline inventory tracking processes for MVP/SVA employees, ensuring efficient resource allocation and utilization, and empowering them to focus on other important tasks.


Furthermore, our project endeavors to expand outreach efforts and maximize impact by employing strategic communication channels and marketing strategies. By effectively disseminating information about our services, we aim to reach a broader audience of military-connected students and their families, thereby enhancing inclusivity and fostering a sense of community engagement with our lending library, making everyone feel a part of this important cause.


In collaboration with Olympic College, the Military and Veterans Program, and Students of America, we are dedicated to upholding the highest standards of professionalism and excellence throughout the development and implementation phases of this project. Again, we are students who want to give back while sharpening our skills with the knowledge we've obtained throughout our courses here at Olympic College. Together, we strive to make a meaningful difference in the lives of military-connected individuals in need of our services and contribute to the enhancement of support systems within our community.
